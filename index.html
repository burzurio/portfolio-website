About Me

I'm a Computer Science student at Portland State University, and a software engineering intern through the MECOP program. I've been interested in computers and programming for as long as I can remember. What started as curiosity about how video games work turned into a passion for building software that solves problems.

That passion continues to grow as I progress through my college journey and enter the start of my career. Right now I'm building my experience through coursework, internships, and personal projects, while continuing to deepen my understanding of how software systems are designed, built, and maintained.

Outside of coding, I enjoy staying active and spending time outdoors. Whether it's going for runs, exploring hiking trails around Oregon, or just getting out for a long drive, I like finding ways to reset and stay balanced outside of school and work.
