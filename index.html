About Me

I'm a first-generation college student, born and raised in Portland, Oregon.

My passion for computer science and programming comes from a lifelong fascination with creating and building things using computers. Ever since I was a kid, I’ve been captivated by the idea of turning ideas into reality through code. That curiosity led me to take a PLTW class in 6th grade, and it stuck with me ever since.

I’m working toward becoming a software engineer with a focus on full stack development. While I’m still gaining experience, I’m also deeply interested in how data privacy works in real-world systems, especially when it comes to databases and cloud applications.

Outside of school, I work part-time as a Field Services Technician. In my free time, I enjoy going for drives, jogging, and hiking.
